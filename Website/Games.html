Games

Rock-Paper-Scissors

Description

You pick a gesture: rock, paper, or scissors. The A.I. will also pick a gesture from the same set at the same time as you. Afterwards, the two gestures are compared. Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock (for some reason), and scissors beats paper. You win by having the winning gesture in comparison to the opponent.

Tip: Having the same gesture means it's a tie, however ties will only return you 3/4ths of your earnings back.

Winnings

Condition (Win) - You will earn 2 times the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Condition (Tie) - You will lose 25% of the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Condition (Lose) - You will lose 90% of the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Blackjack

Description

You and the dealer each draw a card. The goal is to get the total card value as close to 21 as possible without going over. If the value is over 21 the game is over and the person whom drew the last card loses. After each turn, you are given the option to stand which means you cannot draw cards anymore. If both you and the dealer stands without going over 21, the person with the greatest total value wins.

Tip: You can see the dealer's hand, make strategic moves accordingly.

Winnings

Condition (Win) - You will earn 1.8 times the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Condition (Lose) - You will lose 80% of the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Bingo

Description

The less turns you get, the greater the reward. You are given a randomly generated bingo card. For each turn, a number will be called out. If the number matches a number in the bingo card, it is chipped out. If a 5 chips line up horizontal, vertical, or diagonal, you win. However, if the number of turns is up and you still haven't gotten a bingo, you lose.

Tip: Time is money! Go for the more riskier turn limits so you don't waste your time earning chump change.

Winnings

Condition (Win) - You will earn X times the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Condition (Lose) - You will lose 95% of the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Russian Roulette

Description

Enter the maximum bet you want to go for. First, the game starts with you betting 20% of the maximum bet, then increases for each round where no one loses. If the A.I.'s gun goes off first, you win. However, if your gun goes off first, you lose. You can choose to chicken our during the game, however you will lose 66% of the number of chips you betted during the game.

Tip: You will not die from playing this, at least I hope not. Do not try this in real life.

Winnings

Condition (Win) - You will earn 2.75 times the number of chips betted during play.

Condition (Out) - You will lose 66% of the number of chips betted during play.

Condition (Lose) - You will lose ALL of the number of chips betted during play.

Slot Machines

Description

Click three buttons and if all three line up, you get more chips. The higher the number, the more chips you get in the end. However, the higher numbers are rarer and you are less likely to hit all three in a row.

Tip: Do not try to time this, the next value will be random.

Winnings

Condition (Win) - You will earn X times the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Condition (Lose) - You will lose 35% of the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Chicken Fight

Description

Bet on a chicken. They have hidden stats that can potentially defeat the other chicken with one peck, or became so fast that they cannot be touched. Either way, these chickens have it out for each other. The only information available to you is one stat from each of the chickens. Health is the amount of health they have before they are defeated. Attack is the amount of damage they dish out to the other chicken's health. Evasion is the odds the chicken will avoid the other chicken's attack.

Tip: Do not try pitting chickens against each other in real life, that is wrong as well as kind of illegal. I mean c'mon not even gladiators were allowed to outright kill each other.

Winnings

Condition (Win) - You will earn 1.66 times the number of chips betted prior to playing.

Condition (Lose) - You will lose 85% of the number of chips betted prior to playing.
